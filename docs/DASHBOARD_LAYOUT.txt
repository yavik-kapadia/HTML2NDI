COLOR LEGEND
============

Genlock Card Colors:
- Mode: Master (Purple), Slave (Green), Disabled (Gray)
- Status: Synced (Green ✓), Not Synced (Red ✗)
- Offset: <1ms (Green), 1-5ms (Yellow), >5ms (Red)
- Jitter: <500μs (Green), 500μs-1ms (Yellow), >1ms (Red)
